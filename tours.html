Tour Collection

Real Utah rides for different goals and rider styles.

Each outing can be adjusted by weather, group pace, and available daylight. Reach out to customize private departures.

Cathedral Valley Ride

Area: Capitol Reef area

A wide-open high-desert route with iconic monolith views, quiet roads, and big horizon riding.

Difficulty: Moderate

Typical duration: 4–7 hours, usually morning through mid-afternoon

Ideal rider: Riders comfortable with longer days and mixed surface terrain

Included: Guide, route planning, support, scenic/photo stops

Bring: Helmet, layers, hydration, snacks, sun protection

Goblin Valley Desert Trails Ride

Area: Goblin Valley State Park area

Playful desert trail sections with hoodoo views and optional skill-based route choices.

Difficulty: Easy to moderate

Typical duration: 3–5 hours, typically morning or sunset windows

Ideal rider: Newer riders and mixed groups wanting scenery with manageable effort

Included: Guide, route options, support, rest/photo stops

Bring: Helmet, gloves, water, trail snacks, wind layer

Book this tour

Buckhorn Wash Ride + Wedge Camping Option

Area: Buckhorn Wash corridor and the Wedge overlook area

A rugged canyon-country day ride with optional camping add-on for sunset and stars.

Difficulty: Moderate to challenging

Typical duration: 5–8 hours riding time, with optional overnight camp

Ideal rider: Adventure-focused riders ready for longer remote-style outings

Included: Guide, route planning, support points, camp guidance if selected

Bring: Helmet, extra water capacity, repair basics, weather layers
