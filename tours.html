What to expect

  • Ride type options: Scenic mixed-surface routes, singletrack segments, and remote canyon-country mileage.
  • Pace options: Social, steady, or strong tempo pacing with regroup points.
  • Support details: Guide-led route planning, weather-based adjustments, and on-route check-ins.
  • What to bring: Bike in good condition, helmet, hydration, trail snacks, and layers.
